How the Bus2Ride Booking Process Works

📞

★1. Contact Us

Call or email our bookings team — we’re ready to help you plan the perfect trip.

  1. Tell us the date & time, pickup/dropoff points, and approximate passenger count.
  2. Share special requests (restroom on board, wheelchair lift, stops, BYOB, etc.).
  3. We’ll confirm availability and suggest vehicle options within 1 business hour (faster by phone).

Hours: Mon–Sun, 8am–10pm local. For urgent same-day needs call us directly.

Go to Contact UsCall (888) 535-2566
💬

★2. Get a Quote

Request a written quote — our team reviews your trip details and replies with a confirmed price and available vehicles.

  1. Provide pickup city/ZIP, date, approximate passenger count, and estimated hours.
  2. List any must-have amenities (restroom, wheelchair lift, BYOB, extra stops).
  3. We’ll review availability and email a firm quote (usually within 1 business day; faster by phone).

Quotes are written and time-limited (commonly 24–72 hours). If you need a faster turnaround, call us and we’ll prioritize your request.

Go to Get a QuoteCall (888) 535-2566
📝

★3. Reserve Your Ride

Reserve a vehicle once you’ve chosen a quote — a small deposit secures the booking.

  1. Accept the quote and provide your contact details and billing name.
  2. Pay the deposit (card, ACH, or company invoicing where available).
  3. Receive a confirmation email with contract, pickup window and driver info.

Note: For busy dates book as early as possible; deposits are typically refundable per the contract terms.

Go to Reserve Your RideCall (888) 535-2566
🎉

★4. Finalize & Ride

Final checks and day-of instructions so your trip goes smoothly.

  1. Confirm final headcount and pickup times 24–48 hours before departure.
  2. Driver contact and ETA are sent the morning of your event.
  3. Ask about gratuity, tolls, and any last-minute changes — we’ll accommodate when possible.

Pro tip: Text the driver 10–15 minutes before pickup to coordinate curb location.

Go to Finalize & RideCall (888) 535-2566
